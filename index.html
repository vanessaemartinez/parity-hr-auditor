📄 This is your internal Job Description

A Job Description is an internal HR document. It is not published publicly or shared with candidates. It lives in your HR files or HRIS and guides hiring decisions, compensation, and performance reviews.

1
Replace every [MISSING — DATA REQUIRED] line with real information before saving or filing.
2
Share with your hiring manager for review and sign-off.
3
Save to your HR system. Revisit it once a year or whenever the role changes significantly.
📋 Before Filing This Internally Store in your HRIS. Use Arial or Calibri at minimum 12pt. Share with the hiring manager before posting. Update the compensation band before filing. Review annually.

Opens directly in Google Docs (File → Open) or Microsoft Word. No login required.

📢 This is your external Job Posting

A Job Posting is what candidates see. It is shorter, warmer, and written directly to the person applying. This is what you publish on your careers page, LinkedIn, Indeed, and other job boards.

1
Fill in every [MISSING — DATA REQUIRED] field — especially salary range, location, and how to apply.
2
Read it aloud before posting. If it sounds stiff or confusing, simplify it.
3
Save a copy in a dedicated Job Postings folder — separate from your HR records.
📋 Before Posting Externally — Accessibility Checklist Use Arial or Calibri at 14pt minimum. Confirm color contrast is 4.5:1 (WCAG 2.1 AA). Test with NVDA or VoiceOver before publishing. Confirm your ATS portal is screen reader accessible. Offer an alternative application method (email or phone).

Opens directly in Google Docs (File → Open) or Microsoft Word. No login required.
